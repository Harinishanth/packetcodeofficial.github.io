Electric vehicle

Overall global electric car1 stock reached 10 million in 2020, with battery electric vehicles (BEVs) accounting for two-thirds of the world’s electric car fleet. The largest increase in 2020 occurred in Europe, where registrations more than doubled to 1.4 million (a sales share of 10%), making it the world’s leading electric car market for the first time. China followed with 1.2 million registrations (5.7% sales share), and the United States remained third at 295 000 (2% sales share).
